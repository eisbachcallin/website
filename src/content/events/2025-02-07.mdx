Oh shit what’s this?? 😳 we’re throwing another rave and this time it’s for a good cause!
🥰😍 Feierwerk is in need of new CDJs and we’re here to make it happen!

As always we’ll be joined by the one and only MC Treasure Irie on the mic and outerwordly visuals from Visionär 👽 🛸
