A night of Drum&Bass, Breaks, Dubstep, and HipHop at Munich's longest-running underground party:

Brace yourselves for the electrifying vibes of Posij, the Dutch mastermind of unforgettable rhythms and beats that make you want to shake. Originating from the heart of the Netherlands and with a passion for experimentation, Posij has been crafting immersive experiences like no other. This isn't just a night out; it's a revelation to the power of bass music and Eisbach Callin.

As always, Eisbach Callin will be hosted by legendary MC Irie Treasure with visuals from outer space by VJ Visionär.

Don't act like a clown, dance like one!
