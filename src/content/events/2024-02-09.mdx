Celebrating 14 years of electrifying rhythms, Eisbach Callin' is back to set the stage on fire!
After a phenomenal night with Posij, we're amping up the energy.
Get ready to rave with Munich's finest turntablist La Loakaii and the unbeatable Dubrise Soundsystem! Get ready for a night where drum and bass, dubstep & raw power collide.
As always, Eisbach Callin will be hosted by legendary MC Irie Treasure with visuals from outer space by VJ Visionär.
Don't act like a clown, dance like one!
