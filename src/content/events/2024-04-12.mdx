Get ready to rave with Munich's finest bass music crew alongside ! A night where drum and bass, dubstep & raw power collide.
Close your eyes and imagine a place where sound is a river, and its waves are the heartbeat of the city. That place is Eisbach Callin. 

Join us as we welcome Ecliptix and Metadose for a journey to the heart of sound. A night where sound becomes reality, and reality becomes sound.
As always, Eisbach Callin will be hosted by legendary MC Irie Treasure with visuals from outer space by VJ Visionär.

Don't act like a clown, dance like one!
